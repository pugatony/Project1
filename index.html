About Us

We are My Little Princess Dresses. We are a family owned business that designs and makes customized dresses. We cater to clients looking for dresses for Baptisms, First Communions, costume parties and other local markets.

All dresses are hand stitched in our home in Georgia and come in all sizes. Our dresses are made to order and will usually be shipped out in about a week.
